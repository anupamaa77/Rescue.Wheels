Exploratory Data Research

Hidden Prevalence of Vehicle Breakdown Issues

While researching online about the prevalence of vehicle-related problems, we found a surprising lack of data and research on the topic. So, our team decided to conduct a small road interview session to gather data. Out of 100 people surveyed, 80 reported having similar issues. It is clear that people are often unaware of how widespread these problems are. Many individuals have to wait for hours or even days to have their vehicles fixed because mechanics need to travel to the location of the breakdown. Despite the presence of many mechanics along highways, vehicle owners often remain unaware of their availability.

On-Demand Vehicle Assistance

Rescue.Wheels is an innovative platform designed to assist individuals experiencing vehicle troubles on the highway.

Much like a ride-sharing app, Rescue.Wheels connects vehicle owners with nearby mechanics or workshop owners.
When a breakdown occurs, the platform quickly identifies the nearest available mechanic and dispatches them to the location of the stranded vehicle.
This reduces the waiting time and ensures that help is always just a few taps away, making the experience of dealing with vehicle issues more convenient and efficient.
Whether you are a vehicle owner in need of urgent repairs or a mechanic looking to offer your services, Rescue.Wheels bridges the gap and ensures a seamless rescue process.

Technology Used in the Project


Software

Figma




We used Figma to create the initial prototype of our application. Figma's robust features allowed us to design a user-friendly interface and collaborate in real-time. The prototype helped us visualize the app's layout, user flow, and overall design aesthetics before moving on to development.

By iterating on our designs in Figma, we were able to refine the user experience and interface elements, making sure every aspect of the app met our high standards for usability and visual appeal..

GUI

Tikinter

Tkinter is a Python library widely used for creating graphical user interfaces (GUIs). It offers simplicity and efficiency, providing developers with a straightforward toolkit for building interactive applications.
Tkinter's cross-platform compatibility ensures that applications run seamlessly across different operating systems without requiring any modifications. With its customizable widgets and event-driven programming model, developers can create visually appealing and responsive interfaces tailored to their specific requirements
With the assistance of Tkinter, our project underwent a transformation, boasting a flawless GUI that significantly enhanced its usability. Tkinter's intuitive framework enabled us to design an interface that is both visually appealing and effortlessly navigable, contributing to a seamless user experience.

Coding

Python

Python plays a pivotal role in the backend of the Rescue.Wheel system, handling tasks such as data processing, message storage, and integration with external services if needed.
Python has played a pivotal role in the creation of our GUI software, serving as the backbone for its development. Python's rich ecosystem, particularly its Tkinter library, provided us with the tools necessary to craft an intuitive and visually appealing user interface.
Leveraging Python's simplicity and readability, we were able to rapidly prototype and iterate on various design ideas, ensuring that our GUI software met the needs and preferences of our users.
